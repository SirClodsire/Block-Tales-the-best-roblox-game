What even is Block Tales bro

Okok fine, for all of you guys who are too... ah, trivial about the games on Roblox,

(I'm goign to assume you guys all know what Roblox is), Block Tales is an story-based RPG

that is... set in the past? I don't want to spoil too much, but at the beginning, you are

transported back to the past, into the golden age of Roblox, which is around 2010.

 

Now, I don't want to spoil too much of the plot, but essentially the story starts with you

watching the news. They announce that the Roblox VIP convention, which is an event where developers come together

and roblox announces the best game of their catagory for the year. For example, there could be a

best exploration game, best pvp game, best horror game even, ect. They host once every often, and it's a

friendly and fun gather around! Anyways, you exit your house and traverse modern Bizville, which is the town you live in,

and talk to the locals for a while before heading to the convention. There, you take your seat beside some penguins you met

earlier as the show starts.

 

David Bazuki, Aka Builderman, the ceo and leader of Roblox, stands up to give a speech. And not even two seconds this

poor man starts speaking, he imidiately gets jumped by this random dude wearing a dominus and his two cronies!

The man then starts rambling about how Builderman ruined roblox and his audacity to be standing here, talking to the

crowd about how amazing Roblox is at the moment. Builderman's helper decides to give-it a go hero and try to stop the man,

but the ball he throws was easily deflected by the man's two cronies, being a nooblet and a helmet noob (amazing selection, yes yes),

and he also gets absolutely bodied! Seeing this, the two young penguines you're sitting beside bursts into hyristicals, just in time as

their grandpa, and older penguin wakes up. He sees the mysterious man, and remarks how he remembers them before asking you to step up

and defeat him. Obviously, you're confused, especially when the old penguin insists that you've done this multiple times before, and

screams "I'LL KILL YOU!" before both of you guys step up!

 

And finally, this is the part where they teach you the combat system! I'm not going to say much, but it's very interesting to say the least.

it isn't fast-paced, 3-d and dynamic, but instead more of a mix of both skill and strategy. More on this later!

 

After you absolutely wreak these two nooblets, the dominus man exclaims how interesting this is. He said something about not remembering you

in this timeline, but is curious to see what will come next. He then raises a weird device over his head, and the old penguin screams for you to

destroy it, but you miss the throw, and you, along with builderman and the mysterious man, gets sucked in!
